How to Use the Timetable Generator:

  1. Login: Enter the username ("admin") and password ("7") to access the main interface.
  2. Register Teachers: Click "Register Teacher", fill in the teacher's name and ID, and click "Register".
  3. Assign Subjects: After registering teachers, select a grade and assign subjects to the teachers.
  4. Select Grades: Choose which grades to generate the timetable for.
  5. Generate Timetable: Click "Generate Master Timetable" to create the timetable for the selected grades.
  6. Print Timetable: After generating the timetable, click "Print Timetable" to print the timetable.
  7. Extract Timetables: Extract individual timetables for classes and teachers by clicking "Extract Class & Teacher Timetables".
  8. Logout: Click "Logout" to log out from the system.
